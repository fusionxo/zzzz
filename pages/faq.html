Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions.

What is Calverse?

Calverse is a health and wellness platform that provides personalized insights based on your health data. We help you understand your body better to make informed decisions about your lifestyle, diet, and fitness.

How is my data used?

Your privacy is our top priority. Your data is encrypted and used solely to generate your personalized health insights. We do not sell your data to third parties. For more details, please read our Privacy Policy.

Is there a premium version?

Yes, Calverse offers a premium subscription that unlocks advanced features, more detailed reports, and personalized coaching. You can find more information about our premium plans on the dashboard.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. If you have any issues, please contact our support team through the contact form.
