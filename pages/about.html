Why We Built Calverse: The Problems We Solve

We saw three major challenges preventing people from making informed health decisions:

  • Poor Label Literacy: People often misinterpret complex food labels, assuming anything marketed as "healthy" or "high-protein" is good for them without understanding the full picture.
  • Overpriced & Irrelevant Apps: Most popular health apps are designed for Western audiences, using non-Indian food data and hiding essential features behind expensive paywalls.
  • Confusing User Experience: Many apps are difficult to navigate, overwhelming users with data but offering little practical insight, especially for those who need speed and clarity.

We decided to fix all three, and more.
