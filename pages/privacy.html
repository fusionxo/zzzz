Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 28, 2025

Welcome to Calverse. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application. Your privacy is our highest priority, and we are committed to protecting it. By using Calverse, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect data that is essential for the functionality and improvement of our services. The information we collect is categorized as follows:

a. Information You Provide to Us

  • Account Information: When you register for an account, we may collect personal information such as your name and email address to create and manage your account.
  • Health & Wellness Data: Information you voluntarily enter into the app, such as food logs, weight, height, age, gender, and other metrics used in our health calculators.
  • Uploaded Images: Images of food labels that you upload for analysis are processed to extract nutritional information. These images are handled temporarily and are not stored permanently on our servers unless required for debugging, for which we would seek your explicit consent.

b. Information We Collect Automatically

  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous data about your interactions with our app, such as the features you use, the foods you search for, and the frequency of your use. This helps us understand which features are most valuable and where we can make improvements.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic information about your device, such as its operating system and browser type, to ensure compatibility and optimize performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Personalize Our Services: To operate our app, power your analytics dashboard, generate tailored suggestions with our smart algorithms, and help you track your nutrition accurately.
  • To Improve Our Application: To analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, and enhance the user experience.
  • To Communicate With You: To respond to your support requests or provide important updates about our services.
