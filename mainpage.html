About Calverse

Calverse was born from a simple, personal need. While navigating our own health journeys, we realized there was a glaring gap in the digital wellness space for Indians. Most available solutions were either too expensive, lacked a credible Indian food database, or had confusing interfaces.

We are a passionate team of developers and designers committed to building an intuitive health platform that speaks your language, respects your local diet, and empowers you with clarity instead of confusion.
